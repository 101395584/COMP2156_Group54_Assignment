
Renowned for its broad range of programmes in business, technology,
health sciences, hospitality, and design, George Brown College is situated in Toronto, Ontario. 
Since its founding in 1967, it has grown to become one of Canada's top universities, 
renowned for its dedication to real-world, experiential learning that equips students for the workforce.
Serving a sizable and ethnically diverse student body
George Brown College has three major campuses and numerous satellite locations throughout Toronto. 
The college takes pride in its solid industry relationships, 
which offer co-ops, internships, and applied research projects that give students practical experience
 and important networking possibilities. In order to assure student success, 
George Brown also provides a welcoming learning environment with cutting edge facilities, committed professors, and a comprehensive array of support services.
