COMP2156 DevOps course at George Brown College in Toronto is designed to equip students with the essential skills and knowledge needed to thrive in the rapidly evolving field of DevOps.
This comprehensive course covers various aspects of DevOps, including continuous integration, 
continuous delivery, infrastructure as code, automated testing, and deployment pipelines. 
Students learn to utilize popular DevOps tools such as Git, Jenkins, Docker, Kubernetes, and 
Ansible to automate processes and streamline collaboration between development and operations teams.
 Through hands-on projects and practical exercises, students gain proficiency in building and managing
 cloud-native applications and infrastructure, enabling them to effectively support modern software development practices. 
Additionally, the course emphasizes the importance of communication, teamwork, and problem-solving
 skills, preparing students for successful careers in DevOps roles where they play a crucial role in
 accelerating software delivery and enhancing organizational agility.
